About Me.

I am a 1st-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science, Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Baoquan Chen.

I received my M.s. from Tianjin University and I spent four-year undergraduate life in Sichuan University .

My research interests include intrinsic image decomposition, depth image refinement, 3D deep learning etc.
