About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, hosted by Prof. Praneeth Chakravarthula.

I received my Ph.D. in 2024 from Shandong University, where I was supervised by Prof. Baoquan Chen, with most of my research conducted at the Visual Computing and Learning Lab (VCL), Peking University.

Prior to that, I earned my M.S. from Tianjin University and completed my undergraduate studies at Sichuan University.

My research interests lie in 3D scene reconstruction and generation, as well as computer-generated holography.
